Privacy Policy — UNIVIQ

Last updated: July 1, 2026

1. Data collected

UNIVIQ collects the following data:

  • An anonymous device identifier (randomly generated, stored locally)
  • Your TikTok username and profile picture (only if you connect via TikTok Login)
  • Votes cast within the app (emoji type, anonymous)
  • Push notification token (only if you grant permission)

2. How we use your data

  • To display live creator sessions in the feed
  • To count votes in real time during live sessions
  • To send push notifications when a creator goes live (optional)

3. Data sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Data is stored securely on Supabase (supabase.com) infrastructure.

4. TikTok Login

If you connect via TikTok, we only access your username and profile picture. We do not access your content, followers, messages or any other TikTok data.

5. Data retention

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile screen.
